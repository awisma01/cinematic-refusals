About This Project

Cinematic Counter-Cartographies is a research and digital humanities project exploring how Palestinian women filmmakers map, resist, and reimagine the architectures and infrastructures of surveillance and occupation.

This site brings together a curated film archive, interactive maps, typologies of surveillance space, and critical analysis. It is intended as a resource for students, researchers, and anyone interested in the intersections of cinema, space, and power.
