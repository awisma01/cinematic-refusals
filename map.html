About the Map

This interactive map visualizes the network of Israeli checkpoints and their representation in Palestinian cinema. Each marker corresponds to a real checkpoint, with details about its location, status, and how it appears in films.

How to use: Click any marker to view details and analysis, or use the left panel to browse and filter. Switch between "Checkpoints" and "Films" in the panel to explore from different perspectives.

Why this map matters: Space is not neutral. By linking geography with film, it shows how Palestinian women filmmakers document, resist, and reimagine the spatial realities of occupation.
